Our Products

Shoyu Ramen

Shoyu ramen features a clear, brown broth with a chicken or vegetable stock base and soy sauce. In fact, “shoyu” means “soy sauce” in Japanese, giving it a salty and tangy flavor that wont't be too heavy on the taste buds.

Miso Ramen

Miso ramen have a generous amount of miso that gives the broth a strong, pleasantly sharp umami flavor. Miso is fermented bean paste, lending the broth a slightly nutty taste as well. As a result, miso ramen will have thick, curly, slightly chewy noodles.

Tonkostsu Ramen

Tonkotsu means “pork bone” in Japanese, which flavors the broth. The broth is cloudy white and translucent, with a distinctly meaty flavor. The soup broth will contain garlic, spring onions, onion, ginger, and a different type of pork.

Shio Ramen

Shio means “salt” in Japanese. The soup will contain a combination of vegetables, chicken, seaweed, fish, and lots of salt, giving the broth a pale, yellowish color, and ramen noodles will usually be straight noodles.

Ebi Tempura

Ebi tempura is a Japanese fried food made from fresh and large shrimp wrapped in crispy tempura flour. It tastes savory and crunchy.

Shiitake Tempura

Shiitake tempura is made from fresh shiitake mushroom covered in tempura batter. It have an umami taste and it is crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside.

Vegetable Ramen

A deep fried vegetable mix consist of potatoes, carrots, brown onion, and green onions covered in tempura batter. It is crispy, airy and delicious.

Renkon Tempura

Renkon tempura is a thin sliced lotus root tempura. It taste mild and a bit sweet. It has a crunchy texture like typical root vegetables, and the most unique part is that the root has a bunch of holes.

Anago Tempura

Anago tempura is made from a salt-water eels(Conger eels) covered in tempura batter. It is crispy on the outside and soft on the Inside and it have a sweet taste.

Gyoza

Gyoza are are dumplings filled with ground meat and vegetables and wrapped in a thin dough. our gyoza filling consist of ground pork, nira chives, green onion, cabbage, ginger, garlic, soy sauce and sesame oil. It have a soft texture and a strong umami flavor
